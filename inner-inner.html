What's on offer for my organisation

Go Smarter aims to help you make smarter travel choices by leaving the car at home and looking for active, greener and healthier ways of travelling to work or school. By getting more children and families walking, cycling or scooting to school we can cut congestion and help improve the local economy. 

To help ease congestion on the A1 Western Bypass, four key Employment Areas have been identified due to their proximity to this stretch of the A1. With the help of Go Smarter travel to these areas can become easier, quicker and cheaper for employees, which in turn will bring greater economic benefit to local businesses and to the North East as a whole.
